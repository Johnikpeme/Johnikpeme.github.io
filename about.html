About Me

John is a graduate of Petroleum Engineering from Covenant University, Nigeria, with a foresight into the African gaming community and tech space at large. He's currently building Dash Studios, making globally competitive games. At Dash Studios, bridging the gap between African developed games and the global market, growing a community of over 1000+ members in Nigeria, USA, UK, China, Japan, etc.

John is a a data analyst with years of experience in data cleaning, analysis, visualisation and warehousing. He's proficient in data tools like SQL, Python, Excel, Tableu and Microsoft Power Tools. John has worked previously in UAC & NNPC.

At UAC, He was part of the team that led the advancement of systems from a manual system to a more data driven digital system in 2021, leading to an improvement in sales in the south-south of Nigeria with Grand Cereals.
